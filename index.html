About This Hike

600 acres of coastal forest.

Ravenswood Park in Gloucester is one of the North Shore's best-kept secrets — 600 acres of old-growth forest, glacial boulders, swamp overlooks, and quiet trails just minutes from the coast.

We'll do a comfortable loop through the heart of the park, stopping at the boulder field and the Hermit's Plaque before looping back. No experience needed — just bring water, good shoes, and willingness to move.

Rest stops are built in. That's where the real conversations happen.
