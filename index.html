Who We Are

Built from the trail up.

JVO Adventures was born from a simple truth: nature heals. After finding recovery and rediscovering himself through the outdoors — hiking, fishing, hunting, skiing, kayaking — Johnny started bringing others along.

No agenda. Just movement, honesty, and time outside. What started as walks with friends became something bigger: a community where men find themselves the same way Johnny did.

Your recovery is your story. The trail is just where it continues.
